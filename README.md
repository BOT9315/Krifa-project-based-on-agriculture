The Agriculture Project developed for the Smart India Hackathon focuses on solving real-world farming challenges using technology-driven and innovative solutions. The project aims to enhance agricultural productivity, sustainability, and farmer welfare by integrating digital tools, data analytics, and smart farming techniques.

This solution addresses key issues such as unpredictable weather, inefficient irrigation, crop diseases, low market access, and lack of real-time farm information.

🔧 Key Features of the Project:

• Smart crop monitoring using IoT sensors
• Weather prediction and advisory system
• Soil health analysis and fertilizer recommendation
• Automated irrigation for water conservation
• AI-based pest and disease detection
• Farmer mobile app for real-time updates and market prices

🎯 Objectives:

✔ Increase crop yield and quality
✔ Reduce resource wastage (water, fertilizer, pesticides)
✔ Improve farmers’ income and decision-making
✔ Promote sustainable agriculture
✔ Bridge the technology gap in rural areas

🌍 Impact:

The project supports India’s agricultural growth by making farming smarter, more efficient, and climate-resilient. It aligns with the vision of Digital India and Atmanirbhar Bharat by empowering farmers with accessible and affordable technology.
